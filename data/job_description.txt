Job Title: Senior AI Engineer
Department: Artificial Intelligence
Location: Remote (US-based)

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior AI Engineer to join our growing team. In this role, you will 
design, develop, and deploy advanced machine learning models and AI solutions that address 
complex business challenges for our clients. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to 
transform raw data into valuable insights and actionable intelligence.

Key Responsibilities:

• Lead the development of NLP and computer vision solutions for diverse client needs
• Optimize existing ML pipelines for improved performance and accuracy
• Collaborate with data engineers to develop efficient data processing workflows
• Mentor junior team members and contribute to technical direction
• Stay current with the latest research and technologies in AI/ML
• Communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders

Required Qualifications:
• Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, AI, or related field
• 5+ years of professional software development experience
• 3+ years of hands-on experience with ML frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch
• Strong proficiency in Python and related data science libraries
• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
• Knowledge of containerization tools like Docker and orchestration with Kubernetes
• Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail

Preferred Qualifications:
• PhD in Machine Learning, AI, or related field
• Experience with NLP and large language models
• Familiarity with MLOps practices and tools
• Contributions to open-source projects or research publications
• Experience with distributed computing frameworks
